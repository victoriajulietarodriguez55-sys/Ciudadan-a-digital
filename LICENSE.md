    <h1>Impact of Artificial Intelligence on Society</h1>

    <h2>What is Artificial Intelligence?</h2>

    <p>
        Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a technology that allows machines
        and computers to perform tasks that normally require human intelligence,
        such as learning, solving problems, and recognizing images or voices.
    </p>

    <h2>Positive Impacts of AI</h2>

    <p>
        AI helps people in many areas like medicine, education, transportation,
        and communication. It can save time, improve productivity, and help
        doctors detect diseases more quickly.
    </p>

    <h2>Negative Impacts of AI</h2>

    <p>
        Some people worry that AI could replace jobs, spread false information,
        or affect people's privacy if it is not used responsibly.
    </p>

    <h2>AI and the Environment</h2>

    <p>
        AI can help protect the environment by improving recycling systems,
        reducing energy consumption, and helping scientists study climate change.
        However, large AI systems also use a lot of electricity.
    </p>
